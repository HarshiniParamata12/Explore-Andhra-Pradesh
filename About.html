About Andhra Pradesh

Andhra Pradesh is a state located on the southeastern coast of India. It is known for its rich history, ancient temples, beautiful beaches, scenic hill stations, and delicious cuisine. From the spiritual vibes of Tirumala to the scenic valleys of Araku, the state offers a diverse range of experiences for travelers.

Capital & Language

  • Capital: Amaravati
  • Official Language: Telugu (One of the classical languages of India)

Major Tourist Places

  • Tirupati – Famous for Lord Venkateswara Temple
  • Visakhapatnam – Beaches and scenic beauty
  • Araku Valley – Hill station and coffee plantations
  • Gandikota – Known as the Grand Canyon of India
  • Srisailam – Spiritual and natural destination

Culture & Festivals

Andhra Pradesh has a vibrant culture with classical dance forms like Kuchipudi and festivals such as Sankranti, Ugadi, and Dasara. The state is also renowned for its spicy cuisine, including world-famous dishes like Hyderabadi Biryani and Gongura Pachadi.
