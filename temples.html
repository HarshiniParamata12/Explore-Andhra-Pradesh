Spiritual Destinations of Andhra Pradesh

Temples are sacred places for worship, prayer, and spiritual peace.

Famous Temples of Andhra Pradesh

Tirumala Venkateswara Temple

One of the richest and most visited temples in the world, dedicated to Lord Venkateswara.

Srisailam Mallikarjuna

A sacred Jyotirlinga of Lord Shiva located in the Nallamala forests on the Krishna River.

Kanaka Durga Temple

A famous Shakti temple situated on Indrakeeladri Hill in Vijayawada.

Simhachalam Temple

An ancient temple near Visakhapatnam dedicated to Lord Narasimha.

Ahobilam Temples

A group of nine temples dedicated to Lord Narasimha in the Nallamala Hills.

Lepakshi Veerabhadra

Known for its Vijayanagara architecture and the famous hanging pillar.

Amaravati Amareswara

One of the Pancharama Kshetras, dedicated to Lord Shiva at Amaravati.
