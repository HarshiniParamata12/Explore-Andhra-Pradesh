Major Rivers of Andhra Pradesh

Andhra Pradesh has a rich network of rivers that play a vital role in agriculture, drinking water supply, and cultural life

Rivers & Dams of Andhra Pradesh

Godavari River

The longest river in Andhra Pradesh and a major lifeline for irrigation and culture.

Krishna River

An important river supporting agriculture and several major dams.

Penna (Pennar) River

A seasonal river flowing through the Rayalaseema region.

Vamsadhara River

A river mainly used for irrigation in north coastal Andhra.

Nagavali River

A significant river flowing through tribal and coastal regions

Polavaram Dam

A multipurpose irrigation project built across the Godavari River

Nagarjuna Sagar Dam

One of the largest masonry dams in India, built on the Krishna River.

Srisailam Dam

A major hydroelectric dam on the Krishna River

Dowleswaram Barrage

An old irrigation barrage built on the Godavari River

Prakasam Barrage

A historic barrage across the Krishna River at Vijayawada
