Natural Hill Stations

Hill stations that offer cool weather, lush greenery, and peaceful surroundings.

Famous Hill Stations

Araku Valley

A scenic hill station famous for coffee plantations, waterfalls, and lush green valleys.

Tirumala Hills

A sacred hill range famous for the Sri Venkateswara Temple, one of the most visited pilgrimage sites in the world.

Horsley Hills

A peaceful hill retreat known for its cool climate and panoramic mountain views.

Lambasingi

A misty hill village, called the Kashmir of Andhra Pradesh, famous for cold winters and foggy landscapes.

Paderu

A serene hill town known for its tribal culture and beautiful natural surroundings.

Maredumilli

An eco-tourism hill destination rich in dense forests, waterfalls, and wildlife.

Chintapalli

A cool hill region known for apple orchards, spice cultivation, and pleasant weather.
