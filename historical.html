Heritage Places of Andhra Pradesh

Home to many important historical places that reflect its rich cultural and architectural heritage

Historical Sites

Amaravati

An ancient Buddhist center famous for the Great Stupa and rich heritage.

Lepakshi

Known for its Vijayanagara-era temple, murals, and hanging pillar

Gandikota

A historic fort town called the Grand Canyon of India.

Chandragiri Fort

A medieval fort near Tirupati built by the Vijayanagara kings.

Undavalli Caves

Rock-cut caves from the 4th–5th century near Vijayawada.

Borra Caves

Natural limestone caves with historical and geological importance.

Kondaveedu Fort

A hill fort with strong historical significance from medieval times.

Penukonda Fort

A former capital of the Vijayanagara Empire

Ahobilam

A historic temple town linked to ancient legends and architecture
