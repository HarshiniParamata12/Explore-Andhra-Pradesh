Popular Coastal Destinations

Famous Beaches Destinations

Ramakrishna (Vizag)

A popular city beach known for its scenic views, submarine museum, and evening walks.

Yarada Beach

A peaceful beach surrounded by hills, known for its natural beauty and calm atmosphere.

Rushikonda Beach

A clean and well-maintained beach famous for golden sands and water sports activities.

Bheemili Beach

A historic beach town known for its serene coastline and Dutch-era landmarks.

Kalingapatnam Beach

A scenic beach where the Vamsadhara River meets the Bay of Bengal

Uppada Beach

A picturesque beach in Kakinada district known for its wide coastline and sunsets
